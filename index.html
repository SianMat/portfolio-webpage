Who am I?

I graduated from Oxford University in 2010 with a First Class Masters in Engineering. After 2 years as a graduate Engineer, I went back to Oxford University to study a PGCE in Secondary School Mathematics. I have been teaching in Secondary Schools and Further Education ever since.

I started to code when I came across hackerrank, beginning with C++. I enjoyed the challenges and used Google and YouTube videos to help me solve the problems. I progressed onto more and more difficult challenges, particularly enjoying the graph theory problems, for which I needed to learn about algorithms such as Dijkstra's and Prim's.
